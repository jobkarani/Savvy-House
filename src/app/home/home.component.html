SERVICE PROCESS GUIDELINES

Listen and guide

We listen,understand and follow your requirements before refining them into a concept. We adopt a wow and how approach by presenting innovative ideas and giving our suggestions on how to turn them into reality based goals.

Requirements understanding

To ensure coherence between your business needs and what we do to enable it, we define the final requirements with you. We understand that your requirements may change during the process and we are flexible to walk with you throughout the process.

Design, Develop and Support

At the final stage, our designers will work on your design themes and our writer will write the copies for you before they could forwarded to the marketing department for final approval from your end. Once the deliverables are approved, we start the marketing process and keep on providing you on-going support that is required from our side.
